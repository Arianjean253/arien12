WE CAN'T LIVE WITHOUT COMMUNICATION

Communication is the process of exchanging information, ideas, thoughts, or feelings beetween individuals or groups. It involves the transmission of a message from a sender to a receiver through a chosen medium, such as spoken or written words, gestures, or visual symbols.Effective communication ensures that the message is understood as intended by the sender, enabling, and collaboration.
